GEMSBOK

Gemsbok is a descendant of the antelope that lives in the kalahari and namib dessert of Southern Africa.They are characterized by a number of visual features eg:long slim and spiral horns

It is known as one of the very few species where females trophies are sometimes more desirable than male one's.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

 You can find the gemsbok near the panda's canyon
